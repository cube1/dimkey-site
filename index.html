What Dimkey detects

Three engines work in layers — regex, NER model, and your custom dictionary.

Personal identity

Names, SSN, passport numbers, addresses, phone numbers, email addresses

Organizations

Company names, tax IDs, institutional addresses, contract and case numbers

Financial data

Bank accounts, credit card numbers, salary figures, transaction amounts

Custom terms

Add your own sensitive terms with the built-in dictionary. You control what gets redacted.

Redact now. Restore later.

Every redaction creates a local mapping file — an encrypted key that lets you reverse the process and recover the original data. The mapping never leaves your machine. No cloud. No key server. Only you can restore.
